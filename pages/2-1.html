Conditional Expectation

Some facts about the conditional expecation.

08 Aug 2025

1. It is a $L_2$-minimizer

\[ \mathbb{E}[Y|X] = \arg \min_{g(X)} \mathbb{E} [ (Y- g(X))^2] \] $\implies$ Conditional expecation is the best predictor of $Y$ given $X$ in $L_2$ sense.

2. Projection theorem

\[ \mathbb{E}[ (Y - \mathbb{E}[Y | X]) \cdot g(X)] = 0 \quad \forall g \] $\implies$ $Y - \mathbb{E}[Y | X]$ is orthogonal to all $g(X)$
Example
Find optimal $f$ such that \begin{equation} \min_{f \; \text{measurable}} \mathbb{E}_{X,Y} \left[ | f(X+ aY) - Y|^2\right] \label{eq:1} \end{equation} \begin{equation} f^*(z) = \frac{z - \mathbb{E}[X \mid Z=z]}{a} \label{eq:sol} \end{equation}
